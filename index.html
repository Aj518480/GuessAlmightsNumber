Match Almight's Number Game!

Instructions to play:


Almight will think of a random number at the start of the game.

There are four of Almight's students below.By clicking on each student you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to Almight's number,you lose the game if your total score goes above the Almight's number.

The value of each student is hidden from you until you click on them.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each student.
